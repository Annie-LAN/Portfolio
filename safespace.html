SafeSpace: An Engaging 2D Sandbox Adventure

SafeSpace is a 2D sandbox game where you collect resources to build a ship and escape a perilous planet. Dive into an adventure filled with exploration and danger. Will you find the necessary resources and construct your ship, or will you fall victim to the lurking monsters?

Gameplay

Explore new areas and battle monsters as you journey through this hazardous world. Be especially cautious at night when darkness makes it hard to see and new, more dangerous monsters emerge.

Controls

  • Movement: WASD to move, W or Space to jump
  • Equip Items: Number keys, clicking on the slot, or the scroll wheel
  • Use Equipped Items: Left click
  • Inventory Mode: Esc or I
    • Click on items to pick them up and move them to different slots
    • Craft or exit the game within the inventory menu

Tips

  • Recommended: Use a mouse for the best experience.
  • Sign: Left-click the sign to view the content.
  • Night Time: Be extra careful at night! Monsters that can pass through walls come out.
  • Difficulty Modes: Easy Mode: Keeps items on death. Hard Mode: Resets the game on death.
  • Dungeons: Surrounded by hard stone, requiring upgraded tools to explore.

Development Team

SafeSpace was created through a collaboration between me and three other developers: Kylesoni, Sildotdev, and 0Keyblade0. We used C# and the Unity Engine to bring this game to life. My contributions include implementing the hotkey bar, inventory, item interactions, and music and sound effects.
